Events

"Event" blocks in EV3 are like special buttons that you can program to make your robot do specific actions when certain events happen. They add a lot of fun and interactivity to your robot's behavior!

Observations: Why is "Event" important for programming?

  • -> Triggered Actions: They allow the robot to perform actions in response to specific events, like pressing a button, detecting a color, or hearing a sound.
  • -> Messages between Parts: Think of broadcasts as messages that robots send to each other. It's like sending a secret signal to tell your friend something.
  • -> It allows robot to interact with its environment, respond to commands, and perform actions based on specific events
